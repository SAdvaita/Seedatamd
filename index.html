Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our product

How does the 14-day trial work?

You can use all features of the Professional plan for 14 days. No credit card required. At the end of the trial, you can choose to subscribe or downgrade to the free plan.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Changes to your plan will be applied immediately.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes, you can save 20% by choosing annual billing on any of our paid plans.

Yes, we offer a 50% discount for eligible non-profit organizations. Please contact our support team for more information.
